DivvyFines

A Breakdown of who's getting fined and when for Divvy, Chicago's bike share program.

Charts listed as adjusted take into account the total number of rides for the respective group. These charts attempt to show whether or not a certain group is more prone to fines.

Fines by Subscriber Gender

Net Amount Fined

Total Fines Collected split up by men/women in 2014.

Adjusted

The amount of fines collected, on average, per ride by men and women.

We can see that in this case, male subscribers produce more fines because they use the service on average more frequently than women. In terms of fine/per ride averages, however, women were more likely to incur a fine on any given trip.

Fines By Month

Net Amount Fined

Adjusted

The amount of fines collected, on average, broken out by month.

This one is my favorite! The adjusted chart shows the fine rate on a per-ride basis. You can see that users are less likely to incur fines when it's cold out - and the most leisurely stretch is in May, probably because the weather's getting nicer!
